Calgary

Average Winter Temp

-9C (16F)

23C (73F)

Calgary summers are warm but typically less humid than Ontario, with cool evenings due to the elevation. Summer temperatures can vary significantly day to day.

400-450 mm

Calgary has a semi-arid climate with significantly less precipitation than Ontario. Most rainfall occurs in late spring and summer.

Climate Considerations

Calgary has the most sunshine hours of any major Canadian city. The dry climate means less humid summers and powdery snow in winter. Chinook winds provide winter relief but can cause headaches for some people.

Key Takeaways for Couples

Choose Ontario If...

You value cultural diversity, want access to a broader job market across multiple industries, prefer urban amenities, and don't mind paying a premium for housing. Ontario is ideal for couples working in finance, tech, or healthcare, those starting a fintech business, or families seeking established support networks and diverse education options.

Choose Calgary If...

You prioritize housing affordability, higher disposable income, proximity to the Rocky Mountains, and outdoor activities. Calgary is excellent for couples in the energy sector, engineering fields, entrepreneurs seeking lower business costs, or families who want to maximize housing space and access to nature while building wealth.

Family Considerations

Ontario offers more established family networks, diverse educational options, and comprehensive healthcare resources. Calgary provides excellent quality of life for families with more affordable housing, great schools, abundant outdoor recreational opportunities, and family-friendly neighborhoods with newer infrastructure.

Business Opportunities

Ontario provides stronger networks for fintech and established industries with access to more clients and collaborators. Psychology practices benefit from Ontario's larger population but face more competition. Calgary offers lower startup costs, less competition for specialized services, and business-friendly tax structures, making it attractive for new enterprises.
