St. John's Real Estate Market Overview

St. John's, Newfoundland, offers compelling reasons for real estate investment due to its affordability, market growth, and economic resilience.

Affordable Housing Market

📊
Average sale price in 2024: $369,027—significantly lower than many major Canadian cities [Source]
💰
Average price per square foot is around $204, indicating good value for investors [Source]

Strong Market Growth

📈
8.6% increase in average sale prices between 2023 and 2024 with projected 8% rise in 2025 [Source]
🏠
26% year-over-year increase in residential activity as of February 2025 [Source]
💲
Properties often sell above asking prices due to high demand and limited inventory [Source]

Resilient Economy

🏭
Diverse economy supported by industries such as oil and gas, technology, healthcare, and tourism [Source]
⚡
Emerging as a leader in renewable energy and innovation [Source]

Favorable Market Conditions

⚖️
Seller's market due to low inventory levels and increasing demand [Source]
🔑
Lower interest rates and extended amortizations improving affordability for buyers [Source]
